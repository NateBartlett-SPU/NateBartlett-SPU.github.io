The Amalgame:


The Amalgame is a card game i've been working on since high-school. The game is based off the card game 'King Mao', with many of the cards in the deck being references to other games.

The Amalgame has been my passion project for a long time, as I have done well over a hundred cards worth of art, with each card going through several renditions.
